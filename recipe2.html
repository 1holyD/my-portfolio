Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Optional: vegetables or spices for flavor

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add salt and butter or oil.
  3. Add the rinsed rice to the boiling water and stir once.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the water is absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.
