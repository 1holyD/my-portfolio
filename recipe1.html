Pork Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g pork, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the pork cubes to the marinade and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the marinated pork and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until fully cooked and golden brown.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
